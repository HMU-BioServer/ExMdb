Welcome to ExMdb

A comprehensive database of Exosome Molecules by manual curation from experimental and high-throughput data.
ExMdb provides a variety of visualization tools and powerful analysis tools.

Quick Start Get Tools Get Help

Overview of ExMdb

More than 4,000 experimentally supported Gene-Disease associations in exosomes and blood. More than 13,000 diagnostic and therapeutic biomarkers curated from published literature. Detecting casative exosome molecules from high-throughput bulk and single-cell sequencing data. Performing comprehensive analysis including functional enrichment, network construction, immune infiltration and etc.
